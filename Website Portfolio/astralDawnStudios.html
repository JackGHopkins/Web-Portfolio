Astral Dawn Studios

Director of Astral Dawn Studios

What is it?

This is a start-up company I am apart of with two other guys. We are and Indie Game Studios which is currently working on Dodo Alone*, a 2D platformer on the extinction of the famous bird species from Mauritius.

How did it start?

Back in May 2018, we decided to start on a JRPG called Astral Dawn. Since then we work for a year and 3 months on the game, building it up in design, story and game mechanics, however, we have a peg in it for and plan to revisit it once we increase our workforce, as our dream project was too board in scope. Since then we have work on Dodo Alone* a smaller vision game in order to practise our skills and talents while having great fun doing it.

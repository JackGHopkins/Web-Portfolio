Astral Dawn Studios

Director of Astral Dawn Studios

What is it?

This is a start-up company I am a part of with two other guys. We are an Indie Game Studio which is currently working on Dodo Alone*, a 2D platformer on the extinction of the famous bird species from Mauritius.

How did it start?

Back in May 2018, we started developement on a JRPG called Astral Dawn. We worked for a year and 3 months on the game, building it up in design, story, and game mechanics, however, while it was in early developement we put peg in it for and plan to revisit it once we increase our workforce. This was because our dream project was too broard in scope. Since then we have worked on Dodo Alone* a smaller vision game in order to practise our skills and talents while having great fun doing it.

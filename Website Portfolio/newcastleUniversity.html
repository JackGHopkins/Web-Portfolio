    An Afternoon With The Queen
    [Work In Progress]

    In this course, I was tasked with creating my own game from scratch. This was to focus on getting to grips with the many different aspects of Game Development, aside from just coding.

    The Game is a slant on a Version of "Noughts and Crosses" (also referred to as "Tic Tac Toe"), called "Ultimate Noughts and Crosses" where you play on multiple boards at once, with all being interwoven with each out.

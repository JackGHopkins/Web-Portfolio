Personal Projects

A few Personal projects I have done over the years, outside Astral Dawn Studios

Ludum Dare 44 |
Old Macdonald's Farm

This game was the product of the Ludum Dare 44 GameJam, where 2 different friends and I created a game in 72 hours. The game was made around the theme "Life is Currency". Thus calls, like most GameJams, on your creative ability in design and ingenuity. It was a stressful yet fun experience and this has helped me work under pressure well. I also improved my experience with Tile-Sheets and it is good to get a finished product out there, even if it was a little rushed. This was another example of where learning scope and being realistic is important.

YouTube | Jarkey Bacon |

EPQ Project | Pincic Pillager

During year 13 of school, I decided to take up a research project that investigates intuitive game design in classic video games such as Super Mario Bros and Mega Man. Picking apart the practises of the game developers and looking into opinions of industry experts and game designers of the modern day. From that I created my own game with one level, applying what I learnt into one level, where anyone could sit down and play the game to completion with any verbal instructions. For this project I used Construct 2, and created the game logic, design, art, music and sound FX.

From this I learnt many things, such as basic industry practises for planning. And how to monitor my own work, and keep going even when I find it hard. How to take feedback on a project I spent so much time on, was also a great skill to learn. To not take anything to critically and take the sometimes harsh truth of the situation and deal with that emotionally.
